About

Trusted for my design instincts and strategic acumen, I've built a strong reputation for my ability to translate business objectives into visually arresting, emotionally resonant marketing.

I've held senior creative leadership roles at companies like Google, YouTube, and HBO, and most recently, led the Comedy marketing team at Netflix. My portfolio spans some of the world's most iconic entertainment franchises — from Game of Thrones and Boardwalk Empire to Cobra Kai and Emily in Paris — where I've led award-winning, globally integrated campaigns that marry powerful creative with strategic impact.

As a fractional Chief Creative Officer and brand strategy partner, I work with companies to refine their visual language, elevate their creative output, and build brands that stand apart. My expertise also extends to the evolving role of AI in creative workflows, using emerging tools to push design and storytelling in new directions.
