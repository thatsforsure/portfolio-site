Empire State Building: Premiere in Pink

To celebrate the Season 3 premiere of Emily in Paris, Netflix brought the launch to one of New York's most iconic landmarks, lighting the Empire State Building in the show's signature pink. The illumination transformed the city skyline into a large-scale expression of the series' visual identity, turning a globally recognized structure into a living extension of the campaign.

The moment served as the finale to a week of coordinated New York activations—including press appearances, fan events, and immersive experiences—designed to build anticipation around the new season. For viewers and fans, the lighting became a celebratory visual marker that connected the on-screen world of Emily in Paris with the real-world cityscape, reinforcing the show's cultural presence in a way that felt both elevated and accessible.
