Lisa Adams — Brand Identity

The Lisa Adams brand represents an additional tier of the LA Closet Design ecosystem—a private, invitation-only studio for commissions that extend beyond closets into aviation, marine, athletic, and personal styling/studio environments. While LA Closet Design remains the core business, this new tier exists for clients and projects that demand an elevated degree of discretion, personalization, and a higher level of dedicated creative involvement from Lisa Adams herself.

Visually, the identity system carries the DNA of LA Closet Design—refined typography, structure, and restraint—but distills it into something even more elevated and exclusive through a refined aesthetic that exudes luxury and quiet confidence.
